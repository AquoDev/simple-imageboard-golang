GET, POST, DELETE

/post

HOW TO POST

Header:
- Content-Type: application/json; charset=utf-8

Body:
- content: <post content (NOT null OR empty)> (length limit: 1000 chars),
- on_thread: <id of the thread to comment on> OR null to make a new thread,
- reply_to: <id of the post you are replying to in a new or existing thread> OR null,
- pic: <direct external image link> OR null

HOW TO DELETE

Header:
- Content-Type: application/json; charset=utf-8

Body:
- delete_code: <code received after successful posting>
